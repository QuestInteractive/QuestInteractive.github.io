Who Quest Interactive is
Headquartered in Morehead, Kentucky,Quest Interactive, Inc., is one of the world's most successful standalone interactive entertainment company. Quest Interactive Games was founded in 1985 to create the most innovative and progressive interactive entertainment. Our slate of iconic games includes:

Code of conduct
This Code of Ethics sets forth legal and ethical standards for directors, officers and employees of Quest Interactive. This Code is intended to deter wrongdoing and to promote the ethical conduct of all Company business.

Quest Interactive requires that all employees, officers and directors comply with all laws, rules and regulations applicable to the business. Quest Interactive employees are expected to use sound judgment and common sense to comply with all applicable laws, rules and regulations.
